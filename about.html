Brendan Henrique

Critical CS Education and Learning Sciences | UC Berkeley

Brendan is a PhD student at the Computing, Reasoning, and Expression Lab at the Berkeley School of Education and is advised by Michelle Wilkerson. His research focuses on critical approaches to computing education as well as the intersection of computing and STEM education. He was a science teacher in West Contra Costa Unified School District. He holds a M.A. in Urban Education from Loyola Marymount University and a B.A. in Cognitive Science from the University of California, Berkeley.
